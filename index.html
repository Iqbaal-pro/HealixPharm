What Our System Offers

AI-enhanced Stock Management

Leverage intelligent AI insights to automate inventory tracking, predict stock needs and ensure timely restocking - keeping your pharmacy efficient and prepared at all times

WhatsApp Bot

Your pharmacy's all-in-one WhatsApp Assistant Bot helping patients order medicine, channel doctors, get health updates and contact the pharmacy instantly.

E-channelling

Simple WhatsApp-based e-channelling that lets patients selects doctors through your pharmacy - no direct doctor contact needed.

Medicine Delivery

Effortless medicine delivery managed through WhatsApp - helping your pharmacy reach patients faster and more conveniently.

Reminders

Automated WhatsApp reminders for refills and medication doses - helping your patients stay on track effortlessly.

Live Agent

Real-time chat support that lets your pharmacy staff step in instantly when customers need human assistance beyond automated replies.
